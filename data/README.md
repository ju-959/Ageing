# Data

## Sources

The analysis uses publicly available Global Burden of Disease Study 2021 aggregate estimates obtained through GHDx/GBD Results Tool. Full raw downloads are not stored in this repository.

- GBD 2021 data resources: https://ghdx.healthdata.org/gbd-2021/data-input-sources
- GBD Results Tool: https://ghdx.healthdata.org/gbd-2021
- Projected population metadata: WHO Global Health Observatory total-population indicator, with population data from the United Nations Population Division World Population Prospects: https://www.who.int/data/gho/indicator-metadata-registry/imr-details/total-population-%28thousands%29

## GBD Download Scope

The processed analysis inputs should be created from GBD 2021 aggregate estimates using:

- all detailed causes relevant to the analysis; aggregate/total causes should be excluded during download or before processing to avoid double counting;
- locations: Global and the 204 GBD countries and territories included in the analysis. Historical summaries may also include SDI-quintile and WHO-region aggregate locations when those processed rows are supplied. For projection, country-level locations should be projected first; SDI-quintile and WHO-region outputs are then derived by population-weighted aggregation using `projection_location_groups.csv`;
- DALYs as the burden measure for the main analysis;
- incidence rates for primary age-pattern screening;
- prevalence rates only for causes without incidence estimates;
- adult age-pattern screening ages 25-29 through 80-84;
- age-specific estimates for 25-29 through 90-94 for burden aggregation, equivalent-age interpolation and projection;
- sexes: both, female and male where required by the projection analysis;
- years: 1990-2021 for historical analysis and 2022-2040 for projected output. The population-projection file should include the full five-year projection period needed by Nordpred; for a 2040 endpoint this means population denominators through 2041.

## Expected Processed Files

Place the following files in `data/processed/` for the full analysis:

- `incidence_age_pattern.csv`
- `prevalence_age_pattern.csv`
- `daly_age_specific.csv`
- `disease_system_map.csv`
- `population_history_2021.csv`
- `population_projection_who.csv`

The required columns are checked by `scripts/01_prepare_gbd_inputs.R`. Files may be provided either as `.csv` or `.csv.gz`.

Location labels should be consistent across incidence, prevalence, DALY, historical population and projected population files. Age-pattern screening uses Global, Both-sex, 2021 rows. Equivalent-age mapping requires Global, Both-sex, 2021 DALY Rate rows to reconstruct the age-65 benchmark. Projection requires historical and projected population rows for each projected country or territory, with Female and Male rows available so Both-sex denominators can be derived.

Required processed-file schemas:

- `incidence_age_pattern.csv`: `measure`, `location`, `sex`, `age`, `year`, `cause`, `metric`, `val`, `lower`, `upper`
- `prevalence_age_pattern.csv`: `measure`, `location`, `sex`, `age`, `year`, `cause`, `metric`, `val`, `lower`, `upper`
- `daly_age_specific.csv`: `measure`, `location`, `sex`, `age`, `year`, `cause`, `metric`, `val`, `lower`, `upper`
- `disease_system_map.csv`: `cause`, `category`
- `population_history_2021.csv`: `location`, `sex`, `year`, `age`, `val`
- `population_projection_who.csv`: `location`, `sex`, `year`, `age`, `val`
- `projection_location_groups.csv`, optional: `location`, `aggregate_location`

The three GBD estimate files should use GBD age labels exactly as shown in the scripts, for example `25 to 29`, `60 to 64`, `90 to 94`, and `95 plus`.

The projection stage uses the age-related DALY Numbers and Rates generated by `scripts/02_define_age_related_causes.R`. DALY Numbers are rounded before projection. For aggregate SDI or WHO-region projected outputs, provide `projection_location_groups.csv` with columns `location` and `aggregate_location`.
